## Introduction

Wisdom tooth recovery does not follow a single identical timeline for every patient. Recovery depends on the complexity of the extraction, whether the tooth was impacted, whether sedation was used, and how the individual case progresses after treatment.

## Why Recovery Time Varies

Recovery may be influenced by:

- simple versus surgical extraction
- one tooth versus multiple teeth
- degree of impaction
- swelling or infection before treatment
- sedation or anesthesia type
- individual healing response

## Why Patients Should Be Careful With General Timelines

General time estimates can be useful for orientation, but they should not be treated as guarantees. A more complex case may require a different recovery experience than a straightforward extraction.

## What Patients Should Clarify

Before treatment, users should ask:

- What recovery window should I expect in this case?
- What symptoms are considered normal?
- What signs should prompt me to call the provider?
- What restrictions will I have after the procedure?
- What follow-up support is included?
