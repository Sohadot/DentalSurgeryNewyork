## Introduction

DentalSurgeryNewYork.com is designed to publish disciplined, good-faith informational content about oral surgery procedures, cost logic, and patient decision questions in New York.

## Accuracy as a Standard of Good-Faith Discipline

Accuracy on this site means more than avoiding obvious errors. It means trying to describe procedures, pricing logic, and decision frameworks in a way that is fair, restrained, and useful to readers making important decisions.

The site aims to avoid:

- false certainty
- exaggerated claims
- misleading simplifications
- unsupported absolutes
- pricing language that hides material context

## Healthcare Information Has Limits

Oral surgery information is not static. Pricing changes. Insurance participation changes. Clinical protocols evolve. Provider offerings vary. Individual cases differ.

For that reason, readers should understand that:

- no general information page can replace professional diagnosis
- no cost page can substitute for a written treatment estimate
- no provider-selection guide can replace direct credential verification and consultation
