## Introduction

DentalSurgeryNewYork.com is intended to operate as an editorially independent oral surgery reference for patients seeking clearer information in New York.

## Why Independence Matters Here

Oral surgery is a category where patients may encounter a mix of medical, financial, and emotional pressure at the same time. Marketing language, promotional pricing, incomplete treatment quotes, and provider self-presentation can all shape decisions before a patient has a clear understanding of the underlying issues.

An independent reference must therefore be careful not to reproduce the same pressures it claims to help users navigate.

## Our Intended Standard

This site is built around a simple principle: content should be structured to improve understanding, not to covertly steer users toward hidden commercial outcomes.

## What We Do Not Want This Site to Become

This site is not intended to function as:

- undisclosed pay-to-play provider promotion
- a disguised referral funnel presented as patient education
- a rankings site that sells visibility without clear standards
- a content shell whose primary purpose is to convert confusion into lead capture
