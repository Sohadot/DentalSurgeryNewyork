## Introduction

Queens is one of the most diverse oral surgery markets in New York City, with a wide range of practice types and pricing structures. Patients may encounter significant variation in how providers present treatment options and estimate costs.

## Why Provider Structure Matters in Queens

Because Queens encompasses many distinct neighborhoods and a large patient population, the quality of provider communication can vary considerably. Patients benefit from focusing on how clearly the treatment structure is explained rather than on promotional language alone.

## Cost Context

Queens pricing structures may differ from Manhattan, but the same fundamental question applies: what does the quoted number actually include?

## Questions to Ask

Before comparing providers in Queens, patients should ask:

- What is included in this estimate?
- Is sedation priced separately?
- Are imaging and consultation included?
- Is this quote per tooth or total?
- What follow-up is included?
