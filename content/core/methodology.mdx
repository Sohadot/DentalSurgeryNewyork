# Our Methodology

DentalSurgeryNewYork.com is designed as an editorially independent patient reference for oral surgery in New York. This site does not exist to sell treatment, promote a particular practice, or disguise lead generation as guidance.

Its purpose is to help patients make clearer, more informed decisions in a category where pricing, treatment framing, and provider positioning are often difficult to evaluate.

## What We Aim to Publish

We publish content intended to help users understand:

- what a procedure is and when it is commonly considered
- what factors may affect treatment complexity
- what cost categories or pricing structures patients are likely to encounter
- what questions patients should ask before choosing a provider or committing to treatment
- how oral surgery decisions may differ in the context of New York City

## How We Approach Cost Guidance

Cost figures in healthcare and dental care are inherently variable. For that reason, DentalSurgeryNewYork.com does not present cost guidance as guaranteed pricing.

Instead, our cost pages are structured to help users understand:

- the typical components that may make up total treatment cost
- why pricing varies by provider, case complexity, anesthesia needs, imaging requirements, materials, or restorative components
- why low advertised prices may not reflect total treatment cost
- when written itemized estimates matter most

## How Readers Should Use This Site

This site is best used as a pre-consultation reference. It can help readers:

- understand terminology
- compare treatment framing more intelligently
- identify cost questions before appointments
- recognize when a quote may be incomplete
- evaluate provider transparency more carefully

It should not be used as a substitute for diagnosis, emergency treatment, or individualized medical advice.
